about elsakhra

Est 1985

Our Story

Elsakhra was established in 1985, beginning as a modest workshop focused on the maintenance of heavy machinery and the supply of essential spare parts. From the very start, the company demonstrated a strong commitment to quality and reliability, earning the trust of its clients through consistent service and dedication.As operations grew and industry demands evolved, Elsakhra took a strategic step toward expansion, transitioning into a more advanced and capable facility. This move marked a turning point in the company’s development and enabled it to broaden its scope of services and strengthen its market position.Over the years, Elsakhra has steadily built its reputation as a leader in the industrial services sector. Its early values of precision, dependability, and customer focus continue to shape its path forward, supporting ongoing growth and long-term success.

Today at el sakhra

Today, Elsakhra stands as the largest integrated service center in the Middle East, specializing in heavy-duty and industrial maintenance. We offer a comprehensive range of services, including metalworking and forming, metal cutting, dynamic balancing, welding, and more — all tailored to meet the demanding needs of the industrial sector.

Exclusive Representation in Spare Parts

As the exclusive agent and distributor of spare parts for several global brands — including BYG, ctp, CGR, itm — Elsakhra continues to maintain a strong leadership position in the industrial maintenance sector.

Leadership in the Crushers Field

This sector has become a fundamental pillar of our operations, reinforcing our presence as a trusted name in industrial services.

Strong Market Presence and Versatility

With deep technical expertise, partnerships with reputable manufacturers, and strategic collaborations, Elsakhra plays a significant role in the Egyptian market. We serve a wide range of sectors that rely on heavy machinery and equipment, positioning ourselves as a versatile and dependable force in the industry.
