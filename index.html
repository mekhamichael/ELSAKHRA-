our Overview

Elsakhra is a trusted name in heavy equipment maintenance and spare parts across Egypt. Since our founding in [1985], we’ve built our reputation on reliability, expertise, and hands-on service. From bulldozers to excavators, loaders to graders—we specialize in keeping heavy machinery running at peak performance.

With a fully equipped workshop and a wide network of parts suppliers, we serve companies across Cairo, Alexandria, and beyond. Whether it’s preventive maintenance, emergency repairs, or sourcing critical components, Elsakhra is the go-to partner for businesses that can’t afford downtime.

Our mission is simple: Keep your machines working so your business keeps moving..
